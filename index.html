About Us

MyDigiByte is an open-source, community-funded, client-side interface that allows others to interact directly with the DigiByte blockchain, without the need of a third-party.

MyDigiByte is a decentralized DigiByte wallet, that works in your browser, being completely client-sided, meaning that absolutely nothing is saved on our side. Everything that happens in your browser, will stay in your browser - no personal or sensitive information will be exchanged between you and us.

We carefully designed this service using latest security practices and strongest encryption methods, most portability and the most simplest usability - anyone can use this on any modern device, without worrying about their DigiBytes getting stolen.
